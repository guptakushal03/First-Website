Why is having a business website useful, even for small businesses.?


  1.    Acts as an anchor for your marketing.
  2.    Promotes and sells your products/services.
  3.    Connects with new customers (and keeps existing customers).
  4.    Helps local businesses compete with large brands.
  5.    Builds credibility.
  6.    Allows you to control your online destiny.
  7.    Is simple and affordable to set up.

When should you hire a web designer?

Ohkay, so the next question is should you hire a web designer or just do it yourself?
Here are a few points mentioned which might help you get your answer.


You should hire a web designer if…
  • You have a big enough budget.
  • You want all of the technical stuff taken care of for you.
  • You’re willing to work with someone else on your site’s branding.

You should use a website builder if…
  • You want to save money.
  • You don’t mind using drag-and-drop design tools.
  • You want to be directly involved in your website’s creation.
